Core features
Everything your COLP needs. Nothing they don't.

Breach management

Log breaches, track the 72-hour ICO notification window, assign remediation steps, and generate evidence packs for the SRA.

Staff training & competence

Track CPD hours, file reviews, and mandatory training across every team member. Automated chasers for overdue completions.

Automated chase engine

Daily Celery task scans for overdue items — training, CDD, file reviews, deadlines — and sends branded emails with COLP escalation.
